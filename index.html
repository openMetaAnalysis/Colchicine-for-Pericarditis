Clinical summary: This meta-analysis suggests that the clinical intervention is effective. In patients similar to those in the included trials, approximately 33 among 100 benefit from the intervention (see GRADE Profile below). As compared to the review by Norrid below, this meta-analysis adds the CORP-2 trial and the trials of post-pericardiotomy patients since the results are very similar. The quality of evidence is moderate due to imprecision of results. Additional interpretations may be in the references in the 'Cited by' section below.

We included randomized trials of colchicine in patients with pericarditis or undergoing pericardiotomy.

Methods overview:   Studies were abstacted into standardized tables of , and results. This repository updates a previously published meta-analysis.(1) Newer studies included are listed in the references. Rationale for newer trials excluded may be listed at the end of the references.

See methods for details.
